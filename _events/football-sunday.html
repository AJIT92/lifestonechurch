Invite your friends to join us for "Football Sunday", an exciting video event that will play across America on the day of the football championship, February 5, 2017. The video will feature the move of God in the lives of professional football players in exclusive interviews with guys like Benjamin Watson, Anquan Boldin, and Brandon Marshall sharing their faith in Christ.

Last year over 1,700 churches participated in Football Sunday nationwide and over 7,000 people made decisions to trust Christ during the event! Please take advantage of this amazing opportunity to invite your friends, family, neighbors and co-workers to have FUN at church and hear the good news that God loves them!

Don't forget to sport your favorite team jersey!
